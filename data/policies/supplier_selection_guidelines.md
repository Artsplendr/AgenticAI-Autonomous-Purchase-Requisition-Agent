# Supplier Selection Guidelines (Synthetic)

## Evaluation criteria

- Price competitiveness: benchmark against current market range.
- Delivery performance: expected lead time and historical on-time rate.
- Reliability: vendor quality record and SLA adherence.
- Compliance fit: approved category, regional constraints, and policy alignment.

## Preferred supplier guidance

- For laptops and IT devices, prioritize Dell EU and Lenovo EU catalog contracts.
- For office furniture, prioritize ergonomics-certified suppliers.
- For accessory purchases, prefer consolidated orders from approved catalog vendors.

## Tie-breaker rules

- If two suppliers are within 3% price difference, prefer shorter delivery.
- If delivery is equivalent, prefer higher reliability score.
- If still tied, prefer incumbent approved supplier to reduce onboarding risk.

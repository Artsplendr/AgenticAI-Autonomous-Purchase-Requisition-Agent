# Approval Threshold Matrix (Synthetic)

## Financial thresholds (total requisition value, EUR)

- 0 to 4,999: auto-approval allowed if policy compliant and budget available.
- 5,000 to 19,999: manager approval required.
- 20,000 to 49,999: manager + procurement lead approval required.
- 50,000 and above: manager + procurement lead + finance controller approval required.

## Special conditions

- Any request marked urgent must include urgency rationale in approval memo.
- Any policy exception must be routed to procurement governance regardless of amount.
- Budget unavailability results in automatic rejection or deferral pending budget transfer.

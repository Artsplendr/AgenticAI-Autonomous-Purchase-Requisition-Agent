# SynthCorp Procurement Policy (Synthetic)

## 1) Scope and intent

This policy governs all purchases of goods and services initiated by business units of SynthCorp.

## 2) Approved supplier requirement

- All purchases must use approved suppliers listed in vendor master.
- Any non-approved supplier request must be escalated to procurement governance.

## 3) Category rules

- IT hardware (laptops, monitors, peripherals) must be sourced from approved IT vendors.
- Office furniture (desks, chairs, storage) must meet ergonomic and sustainability standards.
- Accessories and low-value consumables should be bundled to reduce order fragmentation.

## 4) Restricted and prohibited purchases

- Prohibited: cryptocurrency mining equipment, gaming consoles, personal-use electronics.
- Restricted: custom hardware above EUR 10,000 requires security and architecture review.

## 5) Pricing and value-for-money

- Buyers must compare at least two supplier options when total amount exceeds EUR 2,500.
- Selection should balance unit price, delivery lead time, and supplier reliability.
- Lowest price alone is not sufficient if service and delivery risk are materially higher.

## 6) Delivery and urgency

- Standard requests should use normal delivery windows.
- Urgent requests may use expedited options if business impact is documented.

## 7) Documentation requirements

- Every requisition must include item description, quantity, requesting department, and business justification.
- Missing mandatory fields should trigger clarification before approval routing.
